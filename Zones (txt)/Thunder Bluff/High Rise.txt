== High Rise

The High Rise is the seat of Thunder Bluff's rule, and contains the grand tepee of Grand Chief Baine Bloodhoof.
It also serves as a gathering spot for tribal matters.

The new Chief of the Bloodhoof clan, Baine Bloodhoof has become the High Chieftain of the tauren race in wake of his father's death.
He watches over Thunder Bluff, and over his people in an ever-darkening world.
