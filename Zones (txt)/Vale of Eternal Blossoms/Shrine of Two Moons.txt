== Shrine of Two Moons

The Shrine of Two Moons is an ancient mogu structure located north of Mogu'shan Palace in the eastern part of the Vale of Eternal Blossoms.
The Golden Lotus have allowed use of the shrine by the Horde, and it serves as the main hub for the faction in the continent of Pandaria.
The Alliance equivalent is the Shrine of Seven Stars and is on the opposite side of Mogu'shan Palace.
