== Mogu'shan Palace

The ancient Mogu'shan Palace is the last true bastion of the mogu's strength.
Three of their great clans recently gathered at this stunning location to meet with their king, Xin the Weaponmaster.
This mighty ruler has formulated a bold plan to unite his scattered people and reclaim the glory of their fallen empire.
With turmoil gripping Pandaria, Xin's lofty dream of conquest could become reality.
